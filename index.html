About Petrichor


Based in Taipei, Taiwan, petrichor is an experimental studio for learning & creations.

Founded in 2025, Petrichor was created as a reaction to Taiwan’s vocational education/labor landscape and cram school culture. Limited by the context of this existing milieu, we wanted to imagine something different. We believe that scientists are artists and artists are scientists, and that the continued separation of the two limits the imagination of what’s possible.

As we enter an era where AI threatens to displace conventional labor and genetic manipulation will undoubtedly transform human wellbeing, we believe it is more important than ever to think across disciplines rather than through strictly vocational frameworks.

BIODESIGN & OTHER THINGS is a project-oriented program that invites high school–age artists and scientists to explore the world through a transdisciplinary lens.

In this program, students will learn the basics of biodesign, bioart, and speculative design while engaging deeply concepts in biotechnology, AI, and related fields. More than that, we want students to imagine beyond the traditional classroom, beyond the internet’s “givens”—to not just solve problems predefined for them, but to understand the world more deeply, so as to discover what they really matters to them.

They will develop their own project to be presented at the 2026 Biodesign Challenge in New York (MoMA & Parsons School of Design)

致科學家們，


位於臺北的「petrichor」是一座實驗性的學習空間。

「Petrichor」描述了雨水落在乾燥土壤與植物上時所產生的宜人泥土氣息。

多年來台灣的“專案導向學習”在現行教育體制與補習文化的局限下，我們渴求創造出一些不一樣的可能性。同時懷抱著：科學家即是藝術家，藝術家亦是科學家；往往將兩者去做分隔，也限制了學生/甚至老師對可能性的想像。

隨著傳統教育因高度專業化而變得碎片化與職業的思維固化，我們希望能培育出一代跨領域的思想家。我們相信，認識那些表面上看似分歧的學科如何交融與再創造，對下一代的科學家真的很重要。

「生物設計與其他」是一個專案導向的課程，邀請高中年齡段的藝術家和科學家透過跨領域的視角探索世界。

在這個課程中，學生將學習生物設計、生物藝術和思辨設計的基礎，同時深入探討生物技術、人工智慧和相關領域的概念。更重要的是，我們希望學生能夠想像超越傳統課堂、超越網路「既定」的可能性——不僅僅是解決預先設定的問題，而是更深刻地理解這個世界，從而發現對他們真正重要的事物。

他們將發展自己的專案，並在2026年紐約生物設計挑戰賽（現代藝術博物館與帕森斯設計學院）中展示
